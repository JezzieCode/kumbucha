Kombucha

Kombucha, often referred to as the "Immortal Health Elixir," traces its roots back to ancient China over 2,000 years ago. This fermented tea is made by combining sweetened black or green tea with a symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeast (SCOBY). Over time, the SCOBY works its magic, transforming the tea into a sparkling, probiotic-rich elixir that tantalizes the senses.
